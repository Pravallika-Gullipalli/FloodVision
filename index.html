FloodVision

Detect early. Act instantly. Recover completely.

🛡️ Before the Flood

🎒

Emergency Kit

Prepare an emergency kit with water, food, medicines, and important documents.

🗺️

Evacuation Plan

Identify safe routes and practice them regularly with your family.

📻

Stay Updated

Keep track of weather updates and flood warnings through reliable sources.

📄

Protect Documents

Store important documents in waterproof covers to protect them.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Plan

Create a family emergency plan and assign responsibilities.

🔋

Charge Devices

Charge devices and keep power banks ready for emergencies.

⚠️ During the Flood

⛰️

Move to Safety

Move immediately to higher ground without waiting.

🚫

Avoid Water

Avoid walking or driving through floodwater as it is dangerous.

⚡

Turn Off Power

Switch off electricity if it is safe to avoid hazards.

🏠

Stay Safe Indoors

Stay indoors in a secure place and remain calm.

📡

Follow Alerts

Follow official alerts and emergency instructions carefully.

📱

Stay Connected

Keep your phone charged and use it wisely.

💙 After the Flood

💧

Safe Water

Do not use water until authorities declare it safe.

🏚️

Check Home

Inspect your home carefully for damage before entering.

📸

Document Damage

Take photos for insurance and records.

🧼

Clean Area

Clean and disinfect your home properly.

🤝

Help Others

Support neighbors and your community.

🏥

Medical Care

Seek medical help if needed.

❤️ Stay Safe

Be prepared, stay alert, and support others.
